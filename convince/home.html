Which Makes the Better Pet?

Many people can agree that dogs and cats make great pets as they provide us with companionship and love. They even have been proven to benefit both the physical and mental health of their owners. Dogs and cats stand at the top of the list for most popular pets, and because of this, many people argue about which one makes a better pet.

Here are just a few reasons why you should consider choosing a dog over a cat.


1. Dogs are Easy to Train

Dogs are more obedient by nature due to their pack mentality which makes them ready to follow a leader and their desire to please.


2. Dogs Make Good Companions

Dogs are loyal and affectionate animals who will provide their owners with unconditional love, comfort, and company. They will be there when they are needed and even when they aren't.


3. Guards

As pack animals, dogs have an instinct to protect pack members from threats. Owners are considered part of the pack, and because of this, dogs will feel the need to guard their owners.


4. Exercise

Like all animals, dogs need exercise. Taking a dog out for walks and runs benefits both the dog and the owner as it keeps them active and healthy. It serves as a good form of exercise for the dog.
