Family Triathlon

1 pm - 5 pm

Saturday, July 10, 2021

Stoneridge Park, San Diego, CA

Bring your dogs!
Swim, bike, run & have fun
Bring friends & family

Conditions listed further down. There will be alternate routes for those who prefer not to or can not swim or bike or both.

Bike Route - 7 miles
