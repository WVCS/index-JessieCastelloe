Family Triathlon

1 pm - 5 pm

Saturday, July 10, 2021

Silverwood Lake, San Bernadino County, CA

Bring your dogs!
Swim, bike, run & have fun
Bring friends & family

A mini triathlon for families and dogs. Similar to a regular triathlon, there will be swimming, biking and running. Like mentioned above, dogs are allowed on certain conditions. Conditions listed further down. There will be alternate routes for those who prefer not to or can not swim or bike or both.

Route distances
  • Swim Route - 0.6 miles
  • Bike Route - 9.2 miles
  • Run Route - 3 miles

The map above shows all the routes for the triathlon, including the alternate paths. For more clarification and details on the routes, scan the QR code or click on the map above.

The swim route is a yellow-orange

The bike route is a light blue

The run path is a dark blue

Distances can be found above. Rest stops providing water and portable toilets can be found mainly along the bike route as well as at the beginning and end.

Conditions for Bringing Your Furry Friend
  • Must be good with other dogs
  • Must be kept on a leash at all time
  • Must be good with strangers or other people
